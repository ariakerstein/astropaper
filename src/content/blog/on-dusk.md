Took a glorious nap at dusk in the formal living room on the leather recliner. Dusk is a reflective time. I always feel this pressure/race condition of sorts - an infinity of possibilities. All must squeeze into a small opening of time. The solution is to relax. Ask yourself how other dusks went. What was memorable. The solution I realized is to feed those into the current mindset - a Bayesian model. That’s how you reduce anxiety. What I ended up doing was simply being in it. Enjoying the moment for what it was. Nothing extra.
