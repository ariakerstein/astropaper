Inspired by Thoreau’s journals figure I might as well get going with mine.
There is a radio show I always liked called heart of space and the tagline was slow music for fast times.
The times are indeed fast. And accelerating. This is my space to counter this. I work in ai, live and breathe it daily. But I’m a naturalist - at least in spirit. And to lose that feels like a fundamental miss. And it’s easy to lose our humanity in the shrill stream of our phone feeds. Images and clips and outrage pull us away from our rooted common humanity. This is global, algorithmic mimetic manipulation of all of us. Step back if only for a moment. Change gears. Think slower.

Be as a pebble thrown from the shore, splash into the rushing waters. Drop slowly and settle. Let the rush happen. Be still. You’re here.
