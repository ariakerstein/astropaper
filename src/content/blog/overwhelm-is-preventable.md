Overwhelm is one of the core problems of modern life.  
The reason is there is far too much we *might* do.  
There's a lot of tugs and pulls in various directions - influencers, content, shiny things.  
More than there ever has been.  
And we are as somewhat empty vessels. That's useful actually (to sellers).  
It's better to want less, need less.  
It's better to know thyself. For if we know ourselves then we know who we are not.  
And there's a heck of a lot of things we should aspire NOT to be.
